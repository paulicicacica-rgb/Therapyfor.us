How it works

From this page to your first session
in under 48 hours.

BetterHelp makes it simple. No commute, no waiting room, no awkward first call.

01

Tell them what's going on

Answer a short questionnaire about what you're dealing with, your preferences, and your schedule. Takes about 5 minutes.

02

Get matched with a therapist

BetterHelp matches you with a licensed therapist within 48 hours. If the fit isn't right, switch anytime for free.

03

Start your sessions

Connect by video, phone, or text — whatever you're comfortable with. Message your therapist between sessions too.

04

Pay weekly, cancel anytime

No monthly commitment, no cancellation fees. Financial aid is available if you need it.
