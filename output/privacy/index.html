Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. This explains what information we collect when you use TherapyFor.us and how it's used.

What we collect

We collect standard web analytics (pages visited, general location by region, device type) to understand how people use the site. We do not require account creation to browse this site.

The Sarah chat widget

If you choose to chat with Sarah, our support widget, the messages you send are processed to generate a response and are not used to identify you personally. We recommend not sharing personally identifying information (full name, address, etc.) in the chat.

Affiliate links

When you click a link to BetterHelp from this site, BetterHelp's own privacy policy governs any information you provide on their platform. We do not have access to your BetterHelp account, sessions, or therapy content. We may receive a notification that a referral resulted in a signup, which is how we earn a commission — we do not receive details about your therapy.

Cookies

We use cookies for basic site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; the site will still work, though some features may be limited.

Third parties

We use third-party services for hosting (Vercel) and analytics. These providers have their own privacy policies governing data they process on our behalf.

Your rights

You can request information about what data we hold about you, or request deletion, by contacting us through our contact page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
