Questions people ask before starting

Will a therapist actually take me seriously if it's online?
Yes. Your therapist is licensed in Virginia and bound by the same ethical standards as any in-office provider. The only difference is logistics—the depth of the work is identical. Many people find they open up more in the privacy of their own space.
What if I can't afford it, even at lower prices?
BetterHelp works with you on pricing. Standard therapy runs $60-90 per week, but they offer flexibility based on income. Many people start with this as a first step before exploring other options. You also get 20% off your first month.
How do I know this will actually help me?
Therapy isn't magic, but research is clear: talk therapy works for anxiety, depression, grief, and many other struggles. You'll likely notice shifts within 4-6 weeks—better sleep, less rumination, clearer thinking. Your therapist will help you track progress.
What if my Virginia therapist is terrible? Can I switch?
Yes, anytime, free. You're not locked in. If the fit isn't right, you request a new therapist immediately. Finding the right person matters, and BetterHelp makes switching painless.
Is this actually confidential if I'm doing it from home?
Completely. BetterHelp uses encrypted messaging and video. It's HIPAA-compliant. Your privacy is protected the same way it would be in an office, with all the legal weight behind it.
